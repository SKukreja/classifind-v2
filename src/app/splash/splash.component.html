Jobs you might be interested in:

Step 1

Create a profile. All you need to enter is some basic information to get started. Your profile is where people can see your job statistics and basic information.

Step 2

Create a job entry for the task you need completed. All you need to enter is the location of the job and a short description. You can also upload some images to help give the job specialists an idea of what needs to done.

Step 3

Find a job specialist for the task. You can browse through the job specialists in your area or await a specialist to bid on your job. Since all job specialists have past job reviews available to view on their profile, you can make sure you find the right person for the job. Once the job has been accepted by both parties, all you need to do is wait for the job to get done.
